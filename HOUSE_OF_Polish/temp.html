Our Story

About Us

Who We Are

A Place Where Beauty Meets Comfort

Welcome to House of Polish — a premier nail and spa destination dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services in a warm, welcoming environment. We believe that every visit should feel like a personal retreat.

Our skilled technicians bring years of expertise and a passion for artistry to every appointment. Whether you're looking for a classic manicure, intricate nail art, or a relaxing pedicure, we are here to exceed your expectations.

We use only the highest quality products to ensure lasting results and the health of your nails. From our curated polish collections to our spa-grade treatments, every detail is thoughtfully chosen with you in mind.
