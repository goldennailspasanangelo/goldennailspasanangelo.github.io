Our Story

About Us

Welcome to Golden Nail & Spa

San Angelo’s Premier Luxury Nail Salon

Nestled in the heart of San Angelo, TX, Golden Nail & Spa is where golden artistry meets genuine hospitality. Since opening our doors at 3546 Sherwood Way, we have been dedicated to providing the finest nail care and beauty treatments in West Texas — from luxurious spa pedicures and precision manicures to stunning acrylic enhancements and professional eyelash extensions.

Our skilled team of licensed nail technicians and beauty specialists are passionate about their craft. We believe every visit should feel like a retreat — a moment of calm, confidence, and care in your busy day. That’s why every service, from a classic manicure to our signature Golden Ultimate Pedicure, is performed with meticulous attention to detail and the highest-quality products available.

We proudly serve the entire San Angelo community — offering walk-in appointments and online booking seven days a week. Whether you’re treating yourself, celebrating with friends, or bringing the kids in for their first manicure, Golden Nail & Spa is your destination for beauty, relaxation, and golden confidence.
